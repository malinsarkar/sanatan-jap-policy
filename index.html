We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) directly from our users. All your prayer data, mantra counts, and notes are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences.

This app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Specifically, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements.

3. Permissions

Our app requires certain permissions to function correctly:

  • Notifications: To send you daily japa reminders.
  • Vibration: To provide haptic feedback during chanting.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Since your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.
